About Us

Our team of optical professionals includes opticians, lab technicians, frame stylists and a full-time onsite optometrist with over 15 years of experience working in the Burlington community. We are a 2-for-1 optical, so there's never a compromise between fashion and budget. We take the time to personally get to know you and your family, offering industry-leading technology and a wide selection of unique frames to suit your individual style.

01

Our Promise

Eyeglass Gallery is a place for you to find high-quality eyewear, while receiving compassionate care with a focus on your ocular health. Our staff is highly trained in vision-threatening eye diseases and works hard to prevent and manage them. Your health and vision matter to us, so we take the time to personally get to know you and your family.

02

2-for-1 Difference

We believe everyone needs more than one pair of glasses. Buy one pair of frames and prescription lenses, and receive a second pair of equal or lesser value at no charge. Some exceptions may apply — contact us for details.
