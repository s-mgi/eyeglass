Since day one

Family-Owned. Community-Loved.

Proudly serving Burlington and the surrounding area with name-brand eyewear and specialty optical services — and treating every patient like family.

Eyeglass Gallery is Burlington's trusted optical boutique, recognized with the Burlington Post Readers' Choice Gold Award for Best Optical Store. We pair the craft of independent eyewear with the care of a modern optometry practice — because your eyes, and how you see the world, deserve nothing less.

Every face is different, and so is every recommendation we make. Our optical professionals take the time to understand your vision needs, your lifestyle and your style before helping you choose the perfect frames and lenses.
