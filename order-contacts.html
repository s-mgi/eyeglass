Contact Lenses

If you're currently in need of corrective eyewear to see clearly, you may have considered contact lenses, which work to refract light properly onto the retina and create clear vision. When you visit our optometry clinic for a fitting appointment, we run a few diagnostic tests to measure your eyes and confirm you're an ideal candidate, then recommend a lens type suited to you and send you home with a trial pair.

We fit and assess a variety of contact lens types, including:

  • Astigmatism contact lenses
  • Multifocal contact lenses
  • Scleral contact lenses (for corneal disease, including keratoconus, and severe dry eye)
