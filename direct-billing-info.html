Optical Direct Billing

What Eyecare Services Does OHIP Cover?

Routine eye exams are covered by OHIP for anyone under the age of 20, aged 65 or older, or with a specified medical condition or disease. Additional government resources are available to support those under Ontario Works, ODSP, and those with refugee status.

Our Optical Retail Direct Billing Partners

When you purchase glasses or contact lenses at Eyeglass Gallery, we may be able to bill your employee benefits, group health or private insurance company directly — meaning less paperwork and less out-of-pocket cost for you at the till.

If you have any questions regarding what's covered by your specific plan, it's best to reach out to your provider directly, or give us a call and we'll happily walk you through the process.

We work with a wide range of group insurance and employee benefits providers to direct bill on your behalf. Bring your insurance card in store or give us a call ahead of your visit, and our team will confirm your coverage before your appointment.
