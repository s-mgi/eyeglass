Privacy Policy

This page adapts the personal information policy published on the current Eyeglass Gallery website into this concept redesign, for completeness. It is provided as a starting draft only — please have legal counsel review before this copy goes live.

Personal Information Protection Overview

At Eyeglass Gallery, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service. As providing this service involves the collection, use and disclosure of some personal information about our customers, protecting that information is one of our highest priorities.

We will inform customers of why and how we collect, use and disclose their personal information, obtain consent where required, and only handle personal information in a manner a reasonable person would consider appropriate in the circumstances. This policy, in compliance with Ontario's Personal Information Protection principles, outlines the practices we follow in protecting customer information, including ensuring its accuracy, confidentiality and security, and allowing customers to request access to and correction of their personal information.

Definitions

Personal Information — information about an identifiable individual. Personal information does not include contact information.

Contact Information — information that would enable an individual to be contacted at a place of business, including name, position or title, business phone number, business address, or business email. Contact information is not covered by this policy.

Privacy Officer — the individual at Eyeglass Gallery responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy.

Collecting Personal Information

Unless the purpose for collecting personal information is obvious and the customer voluntarily provides it for that purpose, we will communicate why information is being collected before or at the time of collection. We will only collect the information necessary to:

  • Verify identity
  • Identify customer preferences
  • Deliver requested products and services
  • Enroll a client in a program
  • Ensure a high standard of service
  • Meet regulatory requirements
  • Contact the customer, if requested, via the information provided

Consent

We will obtain consent to collect, use or disclose personal information except where we are authorized to do so without consent. Consent can be direct or implied where the purpose is obvious and information is voluntarily provided for that purpose, and may also be implied where a customer is given notice and a reasonable opportunity to opt out of mail-outs or marketing communications. Subject to certain exceptions, customers can withhold or withdraw consent for certain uses of their personal information; doing so may limit our ability to provide a particular service, which we will explain to help the customer decide.

Using & Disclosing Personal Information

We will only use or disclose personal information where necessary to fulfill the purposes identified at the time of collection, or a purpose reasonably related to those purposes — for example, to contact customers about products and services that may be of interest. We will not use or disclose personal information for any additional purpose without consent.

Retaining Personal Information

If we use personal information to make a decision that directly affects a customer, we will retain that information for at least one year so the customer has a reasonable opportunity to request access to it. Otherwise, we retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the identified purpose or a legal or business requirement.

Ensuring Accuracy

We make reasonable efforts to keep personal information accurate and complete where it may be used to make a decision about a customer or disclosed to another organization. Customers may request a correction in writing, with sufficient detail to identify the information and the correction sought.

Securing Personal Information

We are committed to protecting personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, copying, modification or disposal, including through secure transmission and appropriate encryption of stored data, and secure destruction methods such as shredding or secure digital erasure. We continually review and update our security practices as technology changes.

Providing Access

Customers have a right to access their personal information, subject to limited exceptions. A request must be made in writing with sufficient detail to identify the information sought. We will respond, and tell customers how their information has been used and disclosed, within 30 business days, or provide written notice of an extension if more time is required. A minimal fee may apply, which we will disclose before proceeding. If a request is refused in full or in part, we will notify the customer in writing with the reasons and available recourse.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please contact us or call 905.331.5999.
