Optometrist

Dr. Matt Chavoustie

Full-Time Onsite Optometrist

Originally from the 1000 Islands Region of New York State, Dr. Chavoustie moved to the GTA to be closer to his wife's family in Hamilton. He has been providing the Burlington community with exceptional eye care for the last 15 years.

Dr. Chavoustie completed his undergraduate studies at the State University of New York at Oswego in 2004, receiving a Bachelor's Degree in Biological Sciences. He then moved to Boston, Massachusetts, where he received his Doctor of Optometry degree from the prestigious New England College of Optometry in 2009 — where he also met his wife, fellow optometrist Dr. Anjali Pathak.

During his final-year rotations, Dr. Chavoustie was sent from coast to coast and exposed to a wide variety of clinical settings, with training focused on fitting specialty contact lenses and co-managing LASIK patients in Atlanta, Georgia.

Specialty Optometry Vision Care

Following graduation, Dr. Chavoustie was accepted into the Ocular Disease Residency Program at the Boston Veteran's Affairs Hospital System, where he further enhanced his knowledge of glaucoma, diabetes, macular degeneration and many other ocular diseases — working closely with globally-renowned specialists in glaucoma, retinal and corneal disease.

He managed complicated ocular diseases, including glaucoma, macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy, at Veteran Affairs Hospitals in Hudson Valley, New York and Portland, and cared for an under-served population at Boston's Upham's Corner Community Health Center. He was also involved in mentoring and teaching 3rd and 4th-year optometry students, and handling urgent cases referred from the hospital system's emergency rooms.

When not in the office, Dr. Chavoustie enjoys exploring all that Burlington has to offer with his wife and their two young children.
