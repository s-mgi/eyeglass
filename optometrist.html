Dr. Matt Chavoustie · OD

Full-time, on-site optometrist

Originally from the 1000 Islands region of New York State, Dr. Chavoustie moved to the GTA to be closer to his wife's family in Hamilton. He has provided the Burlington community with exceptional eye care for the last 15 years.

  • Comprehensive vision & eye-health assessments
  • Glaucoma, macular degeneration & diabetic retinopathy
  • Specialty contact lens fittings
  • Children's and seniors' eye care

Education & training

Dr. Chavoustie completed his undergraduate studies at the State University of New York at Oswego in 2004, receiving a Bachelor's Degree in Biological Sciences. He earned his Doctor of Optometry degree from the prestigious New England College of Optometry in Boston in 2009, where he also met his wife, fellow optometrist Dr. Anjali Pathak.

During his final-year rotations, Dr. Chavoustie trained coast to coast across a wide variety of clinical settings, focusing on fitting specialty contact lenses and co-managing LASIK patients in Atlanta, Georgia. After graduation he was accepted into the Ocular Disease Residency Program at the Boston Veteran's Affairs Hospital System, working alongside globally-renowned specialists in glaucoma, retinal and corneal disease.

He has managed complex ocular diseases, including glaucoma, macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy, at VA Hospitals in New York's Hudson Valley and Portland, and cared for an under-served community at Boston's Upham's Corner Community Health Center. When not in the office, he enjoys exploring all that Burlington offers with his wife and their two young children.
